   Daily Devotional

                       Ephesians 4:11

           Posted by Brendan Kilburn on November 29, 2023

Sometimes we fail to appreciate the people God puts in our lives

Leadership gifts Christ gave:

1. Apostles (11a)

2. Prophets (11b)

The foundation doesn't need to be re-poured, reduplicated, reinvented, or replaced.

3. Evangelists (11c)

Just because you don't have the gift of an evangelist doesn't mean you're exempt from evangelizing.

A church that loses its heart for evangelism is a church that is dying.

4. Pastor-Teachers (11d)

As Christians, we were made to live under the rule of our rightful King.
