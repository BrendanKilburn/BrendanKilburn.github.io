About Me

My Family: The Lord has also blessed me with an amazing mom, dad, and twin brother who have brought me much joy, wisdom, and encouragement.

I also enjoy singing and listening to Biblically solid hymns and other songs. For entertainment, I love watching Clemson football with my family in the fall (Go Tigers!)
